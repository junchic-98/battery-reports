Batteries versus fuel cells for decarbonizing medium- and heavy-duty vehicles across applications
Nature Energy Maxwell Woody, Spencer Checkoway, Gregory A. Keoleian et al. Jun 25, 2026 10.1038/s41560-026-02095-6

Published online: 25 June 2026; doi:10.1038/s41560-026-02095-6 Decarbonizing medium- and heavy-duty vehicles is challenging, and the relative benefits of batteries versus fuel cells remain uncertain across applications. This modelling work shows that both reduce emissions versus diesel, but battery electric vehicles powered by renewable electricity deliver the lowest life-cycle greenhouse gas emissions in most cases.
