Ligand-confined zinc slurries enable durable flow-based energy storage
Nature Energy Jul 13, 2026 10.1038/s41560-026-02096-5

Published online: 13 July 2026; doi:10.1038/s41560-026-02096-5 Zinc-based batteries could offer long-duration energy storage, yet conventional zinc electrodes degrade during cycling. Now, a flowing zinc slurry battery is developed with zinc nanoparticles dispersed in a conductive network and a ligand that stabilizes the slurry. The slurry shows reversible Zn/Zn2+ conversion and enables durable battery operation.
