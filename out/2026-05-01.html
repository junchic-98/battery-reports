An analytical model to describe self-discharge rates in solid-state batteries
Nature Energy May 01, 2026 10.1038/s41560-026-02047-0

Published online: 01 May 2026; doi:10.1038/s41560-026-02047-0 Internal self-discharge can compromise the shelf life of solid-state batteries. Now, physico-chemical analysis of charge loss shows that the internal self-discharge over time is not solely determined by the electronic conductivity of the solid separator but also by its electrochemical stability.
